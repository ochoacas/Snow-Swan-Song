About this project

Overview

This project was supported by the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's Ocean Acidification Program, and was conducted by a research team at Oregon State University. The purpose of the project was to assess shellfish stakeholders' current and future vulnerability to ocean acidification (OA), and to identify pathways to successful adaptation. For more information about the project, click here.
